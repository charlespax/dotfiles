=== screen ===
'screen' is an application that manages terminal screens. It can split the terminal and have multiple "windows".

screen              (start the application)
Ctrl+a c            (new window)
Ctrl+a n            (next window)
Ctrl+a p            (previous window)
Ctrl+a "            (select window from list)
Ctrl+a Ctrl+a       (previously viewed window)
Ctrl+a |            (split window into vertical regions)
Ctrl+a S            (split window into horizontal regions)
ctrl+a tab          (next region)
Ctrl+a :resize      (resize region)
